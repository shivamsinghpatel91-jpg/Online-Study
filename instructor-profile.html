Professional Biography

Shivam Patel is the founder of Learnify India, an innovative educational platform dedicated to providing free and modern learning opportunities to students across India.


He believes in empowering youth through technology, creativity, and accessible education for all.

From a young age, Shivam was passionate about technology and education. He strongly believes that quality learning should not depend on money, but on motivation. With this belief, he started Learnify India — a platform built to provide modern, skill-based, and interactive education to learners across the nation.


Under his guidance, Learnify India aims to create a new generation of students who are confident, skilled, and ready for the global future. Shivam continues to lead with innovation, integrating technology, AI, and creativity into every aspect of learning.


“Education is not a privilege — it’s a right. And technology is the bridge that connects dreams to reality.”


— Shivam Patel, Founder – Learnify India
